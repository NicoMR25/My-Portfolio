Who is Nico?

Nico is someone who finds a lot of joy by embracing a variety of expereinces, constantly seeking out
new adventures and opportunities for excitement. Whether is exploring a new city or listening an
unfamiliar genre of music, he thrives on the thrill of the unknown.

His life gets enriched by the presence of his wonderful wife. Together, they embark on different journeys
whether is attending an electrifying concert featuring Rock or Metal bands, maybe a quick
holiday in Madrid on summer, or also playing together guitar
